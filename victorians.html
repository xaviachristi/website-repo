The Victorians(1837 - 1901)

    -The Victorian age in British history is named after Queen Victoria I
    -Queen Victoria was Britain's queen from 1837 until 1901
    -She became queen at the age of 18
    -Many children went to work, not school during this time
    -There was a rapid growth in Britain's population from 2 million to 6.5 million
    -England experienced an enormous increase in wealth

Anglo-Saxons(450 AD - 1066)

    -Made up of 3 tribes who came to England from across the North Sea around the middle of the 5th century - the Angles, the Saxons and the Jutes
    -For a long time, England wasn't really one country - Anglo-Saxon kings ruled lots of little kingdoms across the land
    -Egbert was the first Anglo-Saxon king to rule England
    -The last Anglo-Saxon king was Harold II in 1066
    -The Anglo-Saxon period covers about 600 years and Anglo-Saxon kings ruled England for about 300 years
    -The 2 most famous Anglo-Saxon kings are Alfred the Great and Canute the Great

Normans(1066 - 1154)

    -They came from Normandy - a region in Northern France
    -Invaded England in 1066 because they wanted to have a Norman king in England after the Anglo-Saxon king died
    -First Norman king was William the Conqueror, who won the Battle of Hastings in 1066 against the Anglo-Saxons
    -William the Conqueror published the Domesday Book
    -The Bayeaux Tapestry tells a story in pictures about the Norman Invasion of Britain and the Battle of Hastings
